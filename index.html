GUN DEATHS IN AMERICA (2006-2020)

Welcome to my project on the gun deaths in the United States over the last 14 years

This website follows the martini glass structure - where you as the reader can start by viewing different slides, each which will show you a different chart breaking down American gun deaths into categories. It's recommended you go in order through the slides - because the last slide is an overall visualization of every gun death that allows for free form exploration based on categories. Clicking on each button navigates you to a webpage containing that chart. Enjoy!
